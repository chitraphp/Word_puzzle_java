Word Puzzle App uses a method to replace vowels in a string with the dash symbol "-" to create a word puzzle to solve.

1.word of single letter
Input:a
output:-

2.word of single letter of non vowel
Input:b
output:b

3.word of two letters of one vowel
Input:ab
output:-b

4.word of two same  vowels
Input:aa
output:--

5.word of two different vowels
Input:au
output:--

6.word of two different letters
Input:xz
output:xz

7.two words
Input:an the
Output:-n th-

8.String with special chars
Input:Sun *rises in the east!
Output:S-n *r-s-es -n th- e-st!

9.word start with special chars
Input:!hooray
Output:!h--r-y

10.word of one capital letter of vowel
Input:A
Output:-

11.word with capitals and special chars
Input:Sun *rises In the East!
OutPut:S-n *r-s-s -n th- -ast!
